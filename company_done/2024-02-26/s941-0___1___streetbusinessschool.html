About us

Street Business School is a global leader in poverty eradication through entrepreneurship educational training. Our trainings equip women with the knowledge and confidence they need to build successful, sustainable micro businesses and lift their families out of poverty. We partner with NGOs on how to use Street Business School's proven entrepreneurship educational training in their communities, empowering people to become self-sustaining entrepreneurs.
