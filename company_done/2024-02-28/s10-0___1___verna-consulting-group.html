      About us

      VERNA CONSULTING GROUP is a company for professional consulting services, with an understanding of local and international standards, which can offer you the most competent, modern and fast service to achieve your GOALS. Our consultants, consultants and partners can help you in planning, preparing and developing your business, identifying your business management strategy, advancing technological development, implementing current and future projects, improving processes within the company, acquiring financial assistance from various government institutions, funds, foreign donors, international organizations and financial institutions and detection of your weaknesses within the business. The team of VERNA CONSULTING GROUP is always in close cooperation with you, to improve your business performance, to increase competitiveness and to realize your ideas. Our team offers tips for success within the industry, in the field of your work, full insight within all upgrade processes. The quality of services for us comes first and is the key to success in building strong and long-term relationships with the client. The VERNA CONSULTING team, together with its partners and consultants offers a professional service for the development of companies and our only goal is to provide a professional and complete service all in one place.
