    In cars, but only, and especially in EV's, the final weight of the product is very important, for saving energy and for a better weight-to-power ratio. This coating from Electrolube not only allows for a very thin protective layer, but also allows for a very simple process without the need for an expensive selective machine. Applicable by dipping process, this ultra-thin layer repels hydrocarbon and silicone oils as well as synthetic fluids and aqueous solutions due to its very low surface energy. Consequently, this product provides excellent protection for printed circuit boards and electronics. When dry, FPC has a low film strength and is therefore easy to remove with minimal friction, allowing assemblies to be coated without masking. So forget the complicated geometry of connector masking or the complicated and expensive coating process. Electrolube's FPC is the solution.
