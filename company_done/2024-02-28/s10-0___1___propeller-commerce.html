About us

Propeller is changing how B2B commerce is done. From separate worlds, where the sales team and e-commerce are disjoint channels, towards a unified approach that brings together on and offline sales. Propeller is a software-as-a-service platform designed for B2B and D2C commerce, where you have all the data at your fingertips to drive more revenue, reduce errors to 0% with zero-touch order processes and deliver outstanding customer experiences with personalized customer portals.

          During a recent investigation, Adrian Collier delved into the intricacies and opportunities of MACH architecture in B2B commerce: This involves leveraging microservices for the individual deployment of features. Consider managing products or orders using APIs hosted in the cloud. This aligns with the principles of Digital Commerce and Headless Commerce. Where a dynamic and flexible frontend might be developed by a single provider, meeting modern requirements. On the other hand, the backend focuses on intricate operations - performing calculations, processing orders, and handling data-level interactions. This exploration goes beyond mere processes. It's about understanding the reasons and exploring the potential outcomes. Envision the innovative possibilities and new ideas this approach could introduce to our digital landscape.
