About us

The story of 'Kapri' begins in the distant 1995, with the opening of a small pizzeria in the old Bitola Bazaar. More than two decades later, the brand Kapri is a recognized name for success and quality in the field of hospitality in the city of Bitola. Hotel Kapri has 20 hotel rooms, an a la carte restaurant with indoor and outdoor seating, a banquet and a seminar hall as well as children playground.
