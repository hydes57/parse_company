About us

Sinpeks is a private ownership company, which exists from 1990 and its main business focus is representation, sales and maintenance of a wide range of products (mechanization, machines and equipment) designed for agriculture, forestry, construction, as well as general use. The Sinpeks business itself is a family tradition which now lasts more than 40 years and is a known brand of equipment of this type.
