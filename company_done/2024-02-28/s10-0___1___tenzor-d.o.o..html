About us

Tenzor Group has taken a strategic place in the Adria region as a system integrator of technical systems and electrical installation with companies in Slovenia, Croatia, Macedonia, Serbia, Albania, Kosovo and a business unit in Austria. We have worked with more than 2000 satisfied clients across Europe. Save energy, time and resources We connect individual systems to complete solutions for increasing security, managing risks, optimizing operations and consequently increasing the customer's profitability. Many years of experience and accumulated knowledge in our R&D department led to the development of our own brands: REMS (Retail Event Management System), iEMS (Industry Event Management System), Callisto (advanced video surveillance systems and video detection) and numerous own software (Leisure, LPR) and integration with third-party software. We provide comprehensive advanced solutions of planning, installing, integrating and maintaining multiple systems in several vertical markets: retail, industry, hospitality and leisure facilities, traffic & parking management, bank & financial institutions and public administration sectors. Contact us today to learn how we can help optimize your business: tenzor@tenzor.si. Advantages of working with Tenzor Group: • 24/7 service and support, • one point of contact, • international coverage, • installation and maintenance team for a wide area of solutions, • knowledge base and expertise, combined with own R&D and design department.

    It is cold outside! ❄This winter wonderland feeling comes with an increased need for heating and reminds us of the importance of #energyefficiency. Eurostat weather-based technical indexes are designed to describe the #energyrequirements of buildings in terms of #heating or #cooling. Although heating degree days values in the EU decreased by 19% between 1979 and 2022, cooling degree days values in 2022 were almost four times higher between 1979 and 2022, indicating that the need for cooling (air conditioning) increased over the last decades. 📈 How can we help so you can enjoy temperature peaks and lows with no worries about the costs and still be #environmentallyconscious? With our #solutions we optimize #energyconsumption. We have the most experience in #tourism, where we lower your costs & enable the highest level of guest comfort, but we find the best energy consumption solutions also for our clients in #retail and #industry.  P.S.
