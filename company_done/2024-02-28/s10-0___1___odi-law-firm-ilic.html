About us

ODI is a leading regional law firm which regularly advises clients on complex cross-border transactions in South East Europe. With offices in Slovenia, Serbia, Croatia, North Macedonia and Bosnia & Herzegovina, we are readily available to assist clients, without regards if legal assistance is required on a single location or multiple locations. Our lawyers have extensive experience in advising clients on a broad range of matters in South East Europe, in particular on mergers and acquisitions, restructuring and insolvency, banking and finance and dispute resolution. We also have strong practices in competition, commercial, employment, intellectual property and technology, telecommunications and media and real estate. We take a very commercial approach to the way we handle transactions and deliver legal advice. Our experience on cross-border transactions combined with our in-depth knowledge of the relevant industries enables us to act for clients most efficiently and effectively.
