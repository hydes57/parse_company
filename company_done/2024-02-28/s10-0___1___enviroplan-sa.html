About us

ENVIROPLAN is an independent private consulting firm with international orientation, founded in 1990, in Athens, Greece. The company is recognised as one of the leading firms in Environmental Management and Engineering Projects. ENVIROPLAN core field of expertise in Environmental Management & Engineering, lies in the Environment, Infrastructure and Research & Development sectors: - Environment : Waste Management & Circular Economy, Water, Wastewater & Flood Prevention, Climate Change, Biodiversity & Natural Resources; -Infrastructure: Energy, Transport & Mobility, Agricultural & Rural Development, Urban & Regional Development; -R&D and Green Innovation: Circular Economy, Low Carbon & Environmentally Sustainable Technologies and Infrastructures, Smart Development; Our mission is to provide specialised high level, professional services in the international market. We aspire to be a progressive consultancy firm in both local and international level, offering innovative, state of the art, environmental management services and solutions. Our vision is to actively contribute to the creation of a sustainable future by maintaining a safe, healthy, and cleaner environment , in balance with economic development and social prosperity.The firm is internationally recognized for its high level of expertise and has successfully elaborated up to date multiple projects covering many disciplines and providing a wide range of services. ENVIROPLAN holds extensive experience in organising, managing and running Large Projects funded by various IFIs, included but not limited to the EU, EIB, EBRD, WB, KfW , ADB and UNDP, as well as PPP of various types.Over the last 25 years we have obtained significant international experience and a well- deserved reputation by working in various developing and EU accession and transition countries, playing a key role in the development of large-major environmental projects according to the EU regulations and international standards.

    ENVIROPLAN, in collaboration with our Consortium partners ΕΝΜ Α.Ε. - ECOS Α.Ε. – ΓΕΩΣΥΝΟΛΟ Ι.Κ.Ε. - ΛΙΖΑ ΜΠΕΝΣΑΣΣΩΝ, successfully delivered on 19/10/2023 a public consultation held in Serres for the 2nd Revision of the River Basin Management Plan for the Water Division of Eastern Macedonia (EL11). This event represented a significant milestone in alignment with the Directive 2000/60/EC on water, fostering the critical approvals and discussions essential for the future of water management in the region. The consultation represents a vital step towards the enhancement of water management practices in the region, and we are proud to have contributed to its success. Key aspects and themes from this event include:   - Comprehensive Analysis of Pressures: The 2nd Revision of the Water Management Plan conducted an in-depth analysis of pressures and their impacts on both surface and underground water systems, a critical foundation for formulating effective strategies.   - Classification of Water Systems: The conference scrutinized the classification of surface and underground water systems, a fundamental element in the efficient management of our water resources.   - Economic Assessment of Water Use: Participants had the opportunity to engage in discussions and deliberations regarding the economic analysis of water use, an integral aspect in guaranteeing the sustainability of our water supply.   - Revised Measures of basic and supplementary measures, which serves as the cornerstone of the plan's implementation.   We extend our gratitude to all participants and stakeholders for their invaluable contributions.   For access to the consultation, please follow this link: https://lnkd.in/dzgmDqAW Your opinions and remarks are highly encouraged, and please do not hesitate to contact us!   Stay tuned for further updates as we uphold our commitment to environmental sustainability and responsible water management. Την Πέμπτη, 12 Οκτωβρίου 2023, και ώρα 11:00 π.μ., θα πραγματοποιηθεί η υβριδική ημερίδα – διαβούλευση παρουσίασης / ενημέρωσης στο πλαίσιο του έργου «Εκπόνηση Ειδικής Περιβαλλοντικής Μελέτης (ΕΠΜ) και Σχεδίων Διαχείρισης (ΣΔ) για τις περιοχές του Δικτύου Natura 2000 της Περιφέρειας Αττικής». Τα αποτελέσματα του έργου θα χρησιμοποιηθούν από όλες τις αρμόδιες για τις προστατευόμενες περιοχές αρχές (Μονάδες Διαχείρισης Προστατευόμενων Περιοχών, ΥΠΕΝ, Περιφέρειες, Αποκεντρωμένη Διοίκηση, κ.λπ.).     🌱🌍 Enviroplan SA at Retaste 2023: Smart Innovation Symposium (SIS#3) 🌱🌍 We are pleased to share our participation in the 3rd International Conference: Retaste: Rethink Food Resources, Losses, and Waste 2023: Smart Innovation Symposium (SIS), held from September 27-29, 2023, at the esteemed campus of Harokopio University of Athens. This collaborative event was organized by Harokopio University and the Hellenic Mediterranean University. The Retaste 2023 three-day conference brought together a diverse group of professionals, including scientists, entrepreneurs, and representatives from various food-related sectors. The central objective of the conference was to provide a platform for the exchange of innovative research and solutions concerning the challenges associated with food production, distribution, consumption, and waste management. RETASTE 2023 aimed to advance research that supports the transition toward more sustainable food systems on both land and at sea, promoting health and respecting planetary boundaries. At the Smart Innovation Symposium (SIS), we had the privilege of presenting on the topic "Circular Economy Boosts Innovative Actions in Waste Management." Our presentation delved into the potential of circular economy principles to revolutionize waste management practices within the food sector. We were honored to share our insights and experiences with fellow participants, underscoring our commitment to sustainability and innovation.Our participation at Retaste 2023: Smart Innovation Symposium (SIS) reflects Enviroplan SA's dedication to driving positive change within the food waste management. We recognize the pivotal role waste management plays in shaping a more sustainable future. By engaging with like-minded individuals and organizations at this conference, we advanced our mission of promoting environmentally responsible and socially conscious food systems. As both sponsors and speakers at this event, we take pride in contributing to the discussions and collaborative efforts aimed at forging a greener, more responsible future for food resources, losses, and waste management.
