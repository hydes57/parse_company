About us

DictionDev offers proficiency tests for Medical interpreters. We currently offer tests in 16 different languages. Our goal is to improve the quality of medical interpreters in the market. This may mean using one of our tests to measure the skill level, using it to screen candidates by recruiters/companies or to improve and maintain the skill level of medical interpreters currently employed.
