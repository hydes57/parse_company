About us

Savoir Vivre is a premium domestic placement agency established in 2013, based in Dubai, UAE with own offices in Eastern Europe and a plethora of partners from the domestic industry carefully selected to cater the most refined client’s needs. We offer high-caliber domestic professionals who can perform demanding and challenging household tasks in the most refined manner. Our clients are the most prestigious households of the region including Royal Families, Diplomatic and Affluent Business, Hollywood and Bollywood celebrities that we have serviced. Savoir Vivre follows a series of objects to ensure we match our clients’ needs with the right candidates for the position. We strive to: 1. Understand each client’s expectations in-depth 2. Tailor a program to suit you’re the most demanding requirements 3. Select process, interview, screen and run a background check on candidates 4. Provide the best qualified candidates available 5. Foster the highest moral principles, confidentiality and work ethics 6. Follow up with a visit or courtesy call after the placement 7. Offers free Manuel’s of Mannerism, Etiquette and House-keeping.
