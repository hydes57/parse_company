About us

Survalent (survalent.com) is the most trusted provider of advanced distribution management systems (ADMS) for electric, gas, transit, renewable energy, and water/wastewater utilities across the globe. Over 700 customers in 40 countries have implemented the SurvalentONE platform to improve operational efficiencies, customer satisfaction and network reliability. The company’s comprehensive substation automation solution, Survalent StationCentral, delivers advanced control and monitoring for enhanced network performance and protection.
