About us

Jeta Group International is an international business solution provider that offers bespoke solutions to help projects and businesses succeed. Our services include HR management and staffing, consultancy and management services, supply chain management, software development, real estate development, travel and tourism development, and sports management and psychology. We take pride in working closely with our clients to understand their needs and goals to deliver tailored solutions that meet their needs.
