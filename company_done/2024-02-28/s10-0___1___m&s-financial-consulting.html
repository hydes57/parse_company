About us

M&S Financial consulting is founded in 1994, with the main goal of providing accounting services financial consulting and accounting advice to Macedonian businesses. More than 15 years M&S Financial Consulting has been successfully providing these services, developing its team and portfolio, and growing. Throughout the years we have added a number of additional services to or portfolio, such as tax consulting and management, financial management, legal advice and more. Today M&S Financial Consulting provides its services to a large number of businesses and non-profit organizations, both local and international. Mission and Vision Mission Our mission is to become the most devoted partner to our clients, and to surpass expectations in the services we provide. The M&S Financial Consulting team achieves this mission through long years of experience and top quality expertise, improving the quality of our services each day. Vision Continued growth and development of our team, through specialization, training and education of our current staff, as well as employment of young and capable people. In this manner we wish to achieve a level of high quality of financial and accounting services on national and international level, while continuing to invest in people above all, and the improving the quality of accounting and financial management on the market.
