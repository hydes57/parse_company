As a proud bronze partner of SensioLabs, it was not just an event but an immersion into cutting-edge technology's pulse. Our Devristo team was present, soaking in the wisdom shared by industry luminaries, engaging in vibrant discussions, and connecting with fellow Symfony like-minded people. SymfonyCon 2023 was not just a conference but an affirmation of our shared commitment to pushing the boundaries of technology.
