About us

Telelink Business Services is a technology service provider, offering consistent combinatorial IT solutions that serve various workloads in every infrastructure. Our technological know-how is divided into 4 Product Families – IT Infrastructure, IoT, Information Security and Digital Transformation. We focus on developing a sustainable IT landscape to accelerate the advancement of tech capabilities for all our clients. Founded in 2001, separated in 2004 as a business unit Telelink Business Services currently operates in 9 different countries Bulgaria (HQ), Albania, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Croatia, Montenegro, North Macedonia, Serbia, Romania, Germany with over 350 employees. Our human force is empowered through shared values of Excellence, Authenticity, Improvement and Inspiration.
