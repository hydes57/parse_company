About us

Toyota Cyprus (Dickran Ouzounian & Co. Ltd) is the national sales and marketing company for Toyota and Lexus in Cyprus, and the home to a wide range of popular cars ranging from the Aygo X, Yaris Cross and Corolla to the C-HR, RAV4, Land Cruiser and Hilux. With full functions of sales, after-sales and genuine parts business across Cyprus and over 80,000 new Toyota vehicles delivered to customers to date, the company is undoubtedly the local leader in the market. In recent years, more than 5,000 Toyota Hybrid vehicles have been sold in Cyprus. In 2022, more than 72 per cent of total sales were electrified vehicles, showing the Cypriot public’s faith in hybrid and electrified powertrains. In addition, the company has made a series of upgrades in terms of services it offers with the introduction of Toyota Insurance Programme and by strengthening the already successful Toyota Easy Programme. Dedicated to providing excellent customer service, Toyota Cyprus strives to hire the very best talent – offering a dynamic and nurturing environment to work in, competitive salaries, and the chance to be part of one of the world's most recognised and innovative global manufacturing companies.
