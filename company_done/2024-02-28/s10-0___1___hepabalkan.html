About us

HEPA Balkan Office was established in 2019 with goal to improve Hungarian Trade and diversify the foreign economic relationships of Hungary. Our main task is to promote export of Hungarian Products, Services and Capital. Balkan Office is authorized by the Hungarian Government to find investors and exporters for Hungary and is representative of Serbia, North Macedonia, Montenegro, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Albania and Kosovo in the Hungarian Export Promotion Agency ( HEPA). Our headquarter is located in Belgrade, Serbia with additional offices in Skopje, North Macedonia and Podgorica, Montenegro. HEPA Balkan Office’s operations are performed by Invictus Media d.o.o. in Serbia, Ekoclub dooel in North Macedonia and COMP-COMERC d.o.o. in Montenegro.
