About us

A software quality assurance company helping start-ups and Fortune 500 companies worldwide to enhance their products by providing a wide range of testing services, custom tools and even leading internal teams. We specialize in software testing and quality assurance and we develop advanced custom solutions for security, battery and data usage testing of mobile apps, video/audio quality assessment for VoIP/communications apps and products for automated backend API as well as web load/performance testing. We are a team of 500+ highly motivated and experienced professionals focusing on software testing and development. Most of our test engineers have International Software Testing Qualifications Board (ISTQB) certifications. This guarantees a very high level of expertise in software testing according to the best industry standards and high service quality.
