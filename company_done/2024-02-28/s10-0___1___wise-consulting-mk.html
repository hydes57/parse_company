About us

WISE CONSULTING is a company established after 18 years of experience on high management positions in milk and dairy industry, as well as organizational management in the manufacturing industry of the founders and the team of experts, with goal to provide its customers with high quality service in the field of: - Optimization, digitalization and lifecycle management for the process. - Organizational and Business Development (creating organizational structure and procedures. - Human Resources-the development of human potential through specialized trainings created separately for each company according to the NLP methodology. - Finance in Business-preparation of the necessary documentation for the access to finance, IPARD and direct EU programs Horizon Europe and Erasmus. Depending on a need for specialized knowledge in a specific area, the company additionally engages external collaborators - consultants with one and only goal to get the client or project as detailed and as specified possible necessary data, which will be used for proper decision making. The motivation for our company is to make decisions based on previously done detailed analysis and research and thus to reduce the existing risk, the unwanted loss of funds in unprofitable and inefficient projects. That is what inspires our company to give their maximum in every project and this is one of the ways to help the business sector in our country to improve its efficiency.
