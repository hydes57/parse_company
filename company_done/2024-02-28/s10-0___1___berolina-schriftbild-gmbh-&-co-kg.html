About us

For over 50 years, berolina has been the specialist for intelligent services and goods, concerning all aspects of Print & Document Management. Being manufacturer-independent and owner-managed, we develop customer-specific solutions, so that a optimal concept, in every respect, is implemented for all printing and document processes. We optimize the flow of documents, uncover potential savings and simultaneously protect the environment with our products and services. With us, you receive your printer or copier made to measure. Print without complications, without stress, without environmental impact. Consumables from our own development. Designed in Germany. Software that digitizes processes automatically and saves your documents. With experience and a strong service orientation, we insure that everything runs smoothly for you. We free your time for your core business.
