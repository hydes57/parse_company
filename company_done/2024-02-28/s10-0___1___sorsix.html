About us

Since 2012, Sorsix has deployed its e-health platform known as Pinga; an integrated health information system that has revolutionised the provision and coordination of health care. For countries that have integrated Pinga such as Macedonia and Serbia, waiting lists have disappeared within six months, and their systems have doubled their capacity. With users in Australia, New Zealand and Europe, Sorsix’s mission is about solving human health. Our Pinga platform manages 10M+ patients, as well as storing 56M+ patient-year records. Pinga’s modules have been deployed in 330 major clinical centres, for 110K+ clinical resources and currently 55K+ daily users. Pinga is a ‘health operating system,’ enabling health processes to be executed across multiple systems and providing interfaces for everything in between. - Electronic Health Records (EHRs) - Online booking - PMS and EMR - Electronic Prescriptions - Electronic Referrals - Hospital Stay and Surgery Information System (HIS) - Laboratory Information System (LIS) - Radiology Information System (RIS) Within our cloud-based platform, we have Snapscan; our online radiology patient referral and booking system. Snapscan reduces the number of patients who never act on a referral, increases revenue, delivers cost savings, integrates with both general practitioners, specialist software and much more. With Sorsix, you can replace dozens of software products with a single seamless solution.
