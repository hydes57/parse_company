About us

Since 2012, Sorsix has deployed its e-health platform known as Pinga; an integrated health information system that has revolutionised the provision and coordination of health care. For countries that have integrated Pinga such as Macedonia and Serbia, waiting lists have disappeared within six months, and their systems have doubled their capacity. With users in Australia, New Zealand and Europe, Sorsix‚Äôs mission is about solving human health. Our Pinga platform manages 10M+ patients, as well as storing 56M+ patient-year records.
