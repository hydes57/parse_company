About us

MODIUM is an architecture practice creating various scale buildings, nationally and internationally. We are a collaborative team of architects, innovators and interior designers based in Prishtina - Kosovo, Tirana - Albania, Skopje - North Madeconia and Sankt Moritz - Switzerland. With an understanding that space is a spiritual dimension of architecture, the core of our design practice focuses on merging creative and integrated design processes by utilizing an intelligent applications of construction techniques. Through various spatial articulations, capitalizing on potential of form and time, our designs enhance the experience and quality of life of their inhabitants. Being a contributor to a larger context of how human experience is impacted from the surrounding space, our value proposition is to develop building systems with an adequacy to enrich and uplift the energy of human interaction and connectivity. Our portfolio is much diversified and since 2007 we have been privileged to service various architectural sectors including residential, commercial, education and interior design. With overpopulation and non-renewable resources, currently our work is mostly focused on modular design to provide innovative architectural solutions to the urgent demands and issues of our time
