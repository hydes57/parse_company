About us

Marketing365 is the leading source of news and networking platform for marketing, PR and media professionals from Macedonia. Marketing365 is voted as a: * Macedonia's "Best Blog" (year 2011 and 2015), * Most influential blog in Balkan coming from Macedonia (2011), * Second placed for "Best business-info portal in Macedonia" (2014) Marketing365 is one of the founders of "Balkan Marketing Network" , platform which aim is developing of marketing industry in Balkan region and enabling networking of advertisers, mediums and marketing agencies from Macedonia, Serbia, Croatia, BiH, Montenegro and Slovenia. Marketing365 is a official representative for Lions Festivals (Cannes Lions,Lions Health, Lions Innovation, Eurobest, Spikes Asia, Dubai Lynx, Asian Marketing Effectiveness and Strategy Awards) in Republic of Macedonia. Since the start of 2017, Marketing365 is a active member of American Chamber of Commerce in Macedonia with aim to build better business environment in the country.
