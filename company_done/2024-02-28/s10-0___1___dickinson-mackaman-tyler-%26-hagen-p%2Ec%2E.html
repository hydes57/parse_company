About us

Dickinson, Mackaman, Tyler & Hagen, P.C. is a general practice law firm with offices in Des Moines, Iowa. Dickinson attorneys provide a full range of legal and business counseling services to a client base that includes large corporations, small businesses, governmental entities and not-for-profit organizations with interests in Iowa.
