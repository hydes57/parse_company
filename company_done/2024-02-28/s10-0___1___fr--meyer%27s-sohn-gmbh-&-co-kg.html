About us

Fr. Meyer’s Sohn is a family-owned global forwarding company. With our 125 yearsof experience we get you moving. Personally, digitally, sustainably. As a global freight forwarding company, we are a passionate service provider. We don’t want to be the biggest, but the best in what we do. With our knowledge and experience, you benefit from customized logistics solutions, sustainable services and the analysis and optimization of your supply chains. Tailored customer solutions across all modes of transport are also part of our product portfolio just like our wide range of additional services. More than 1,000 transport and logistics specialists at over 50 locations worldwide gladly take care of your requests and matters. Additionally we apply market leading digital technologies to increase the value of your supply chains in long term. We take you further and make it uncomplicated for you – with our digital solutions and in direct dialog.
