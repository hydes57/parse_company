About us

The World Spirit of the Game Foundation was formed to help individuals and organizations take personal accountability for their impact on the world. We are a global coalition of NGOs, entrepreneurs, coaches/consultants, university groups, and average citizens all working for: A more equal society A more sustainable way of doing business, And overall to create a "new definition of winning." across all areas of society. We are organized through 4 different Pillars: 1) The Impactful Networks - Helping individuals and organizations to maximize their positive Social Impact through networking, peer groups, as well as online and in-person training and events. 2) Impact Coaching Coalition - Offering a full suites of coaching/consulting services to corporations, SMEs, start-ups, universities and school districts. 3) NGO support and networking - We are establishing a global network of partner NGOs at the National and City level to help solve societal issues through the lens of Personal Accountability. 4) Global Social Movement - based on the world's first "crowd-sourced life philosophy which brings together people from all nations, belief systems, ethnicities, and socio-economic strata, all working together to live life according to a "new definition of winning”. This page represents our network across the diverse nations of South-Eastern Europe.

    Humanity is older than we sometimes remember. We often talk about the Golden Years, about the French Revolution, the English Industrial Revolution, the Baroque, the Renaissance and the Middle Ages, but we often forget to talk about our ancestors from Antiquity, those from long ago, those who have let us proofs of a high quality of culture, of of art, of freedom. Together with our honored guest Mrs. Carla Ionescu, a high connoisseur of Ancient Culture and an University Professor, we propose to discuss the life of women in Antiquity. Who were the predecessors of Sara Khatun, Lucrezia Tornabuoni, Isabella d'Este, Elizabeth I of England, Christina of Sweden, Émilie du Châtelet, Marie Curie, Gabriela Mistral, Sirimavo Bandaranaike and other women who managed to influence the era we live in? Were the powerful women of our era a novelty, or just a shadow of the great women of the ancient era? Between the current era and the past, between women and their ancient ancestors, a lesson in an open dialogue, a historical and social analysis. We invite you to join us on May 1, from 6pm UTC+1 (London Time). Spend the ancient day of Beltane with us and let's talk about present and our beginnings. 👀

    The Impactful Women's Network - TIWN.org

    957 followers

    10mo Edited

    Private life and profession are two parallel plans that each of us maintains throughout life, but what happens when these plans intersect, when we begin to lose freedom in favor of work, and the stress becomes our companion at every moment of the day? The stress is the main reason for nervous diseases for people under 40, according to the World Health Organization International Report 2019, according to the same report 7 out of 10 adults end up in severe depression and 2 out of 10 people commit suicide due to the lack of balance between free time and classes for work. On April 10, we will discuss how we can maintain a healthy and favorable balance between our private life and our profession, respectively our working hours. Together with the two invited experts, Katarzyna Dorosz and Lucia Dumenica, we will establish the boundaries that we must offer ourselves and the way in which we can reclaim our lives control. On April 10 from 6 pm (GMT +1 - London time) will be the time for another TIWN Online Conference Join and discover the balance of your own life! 📣
