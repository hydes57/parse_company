About us

The Regional Programme for Local Democracy in the Western Balkans (ReLOaD) is funded by the European Union (EU) and implemented by the United Nations Development Program (UNDP), in the Western Balkans - Albania, Bosnia and Herzegovina (BiH), Kosovo*, North Macedonia, Montenegro and Serbia. The overall objective of the project is to strengthen participatory democracies and EU integration in the Western Balkans by empowering civil society and encouraging young people to actively take part in the decision-making process, and by improving a supportive legal and financial environment for civil society. __________________________ * Kosovo (SCR 1244/1999)

      Through the #LODMethodology, Albanian municipalities are redefining collaboration with #CivilSociety. UNDP Albania developed a story that gives more insights into the good results: https://shorturl.at/cmuD9 Here are the key highlights: 🔵 Transparency and Accountability: To ensure fairness and accountability, LOD emphasizes #transparency and active #CSO involvement in project selection. 🔵 Municipal Adoption: Municipalities such as Tirana and Shkodra officially approve LOD, fostering trust between local governments and CSOs. 🔵 Optimizing Public Funds: LOD optimizes public funds utilization, thereby contributing to peaceful societies and aligning with Albania's EU integration. 🔵 Capacity-building: ReLOaD provides municipalities with LOD methodology tools, enhancing their ability to plan public funds transparently and support civil society initiatives. 🔵 Real Impact: Beneficiary municipalities, such as Kavaja and Lushnja, applaud the LOD for securing funds for projects that align with local priorities and #EUintegration. 🔵 CSO Empowerment: Under LOD methodology, #YouthInitiatives and CSO staff training thrive, enhancing capacity and proposal quality. 🔵 Mentoring for Growth: Tirana and Shkodra serve as mentors to other municipalities, ensuring high-quality project planning and execution. 🔵 LOD methodology gains acceptance at the national level, with increased CSO project proposals demonstrating increased interest in public funds. 🔵 Regional Impact: The EU-funded ReLOaD programme fosters collaboration, which contributes to increased trust in #LocalGovernment institutions. 🔵 Catalyzing Future Collaborations: Beyond ReLOaD, LOD methodology has the potential to catalyze active engagement and collaboration for #SustainableLocalDevelopment in Albania. #WesternBalkans #LocalDemocracy #EuropeanUnion #UNDP #UNDPEUPartnership
