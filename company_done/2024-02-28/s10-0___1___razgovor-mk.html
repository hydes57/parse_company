About us

We are growing a mental healthcare marketplace with the aim to streamline the whole therapy experience. Our mission is to make self-discovery and self-care simple, clear, and available to everyone around the world. We envision a world where everyone is centred on the continuous discovery and understanding of their essence. To work towards this world, we build tools for psychologists and their clients that make therapy more accessible, engaging and affordable.
