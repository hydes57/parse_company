    Why you should try pitching in your connection request message? This might sound irritating but hear me out. We’ve had one of the best results in booking meetings for us and our client’s campaigns using this approach. It’s very simple: try to explain how you help your target audience with your service with a very specific proposition. Here’s an example: Hi [Name], I help [niche] like [company name] get [value proposition]. If this is of interest to you, I’d be happy to connect and exchange ideas about [topic]. Best, [your name]. We’ve used the same approach for our campaigns for 3 years straight. Yep! That’s right, and it gets us results till date. The outcome? 2-3 meetings booked per week, plus we’ve generated more than 25 leads in a single month for one of the clients.
