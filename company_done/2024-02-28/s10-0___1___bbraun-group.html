About us

B. Braun is one of the world's leading medical technology companies. With over 65,000 employees, the family-owned company is a true partner, developing smart solutions and setting standards to drive advancements in health care. In 2022, the B. Braun Group generated sales of € 8.5 billion. You want to protect and improve the health of people around the world? Then you need a work environment that makes it easy for you to give your best. We bring together people in a collaborative culture. We encourage them to be open-minded, learn from each other and create new solutions together. In a time of rapid technological change, we constantly strive to enhance the solutions we provide. This is why we promote innovative ways of thinking and accountability.     We are thrilled to share that B. Braun India conducted a scientific training workshop, “CHEMOPORT – MASTERING CARE AND ACCESS WORKSHOP” dated 24th February 2024. This workshop was aimed at providing participants with comprehensive training on techniques and complication management.   The workshop was attended by 40+ #oncologists from across the country and live relay was attended by 150+ Doctors, Nursing Team, Resident Doctors at 30+ centers across major institutes of India.   This 6-hour workshop was divided in 2 phases: ·      Live Workshops: Chemoport implantation amongst adult & pediatric patients ·      Scientific Discussions: Insertion, complication management & maintenance   The scientific deliberations were led by Dr Ramakant Deshpande, Senior Onco Surgeon, Executive Chairman, Asian Cancer Institute, Dr Shishir Shetty, Professor & Head Surgical Oncology, Medicover Hospital, Dr. Shirish Borkar, Chief Consultant Cardiovascular & Thoracic Surgeon, GMC, Dr Anupama Borkar, HOD, Medical Oncology, GMC, and Dr. Shekhar Salkar, Senior Consultant Onco Surgeon, Manipal Hospital.   The live workshops were conducted by renowned surgeons Dr Ramakant Deshpande, Senior Onco Surgeon, Executive Chairman, Asian Cancer Institute, Dr Sajid Qureshi, Professor, Division of Paediatric Surgical Oncology, Tata Memorial Hospital, and Dr Ameenuddin Khan, Consultant, Surgical Oncology, Fortis Hospital.   The Scientific Discussions involved all the aspects of #Chemoports usage in Oncology clinical practice. It was delightful to see doctors #sharingexpertise on complication management associated with Chemoports.   We extend our heartfelt gratitude and thanks to Goa Medical College (GMC), all the Programme Directors, Scientific Coordinators, Scientific Committee members and Participants for their active engagement and enthusiasm throughout the workshop. This event was CME accredited by Goa Medical Council and Goa Medical College. We really appreciate their dedication in advancing skills and commitment towards protecting and improving the health of people around the world.   #bbraun is committed towards facilitation of training of clinicians in the latest technology for cancer care and treatment.
