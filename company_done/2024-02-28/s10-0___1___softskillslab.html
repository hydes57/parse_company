About us

Why Soft Skills Lab? The design of teaching curricula everywhere in the world currently is based on fulfilling the 21st century skills. Due to the rapid change and unpredictable challenges, these trends are oriented towards some basic and dynamic competencies that hold their place with every change of known as soft skills. With our methodology and tools, we go one step further in the development of soft skills. Soft Skills Lab based on the functional structure of "together sets" in an effective manner, prepares training scenarios from different fields and topics for conferences, workshops, seminars, social activities, product development, etc.
