        At the webinar "Online kupci u 2023. i očekivanja u 2024", Kristijan Gregoric (Valicon) and Igor Martinović (Jeftinije!) presented new results of the Croatian Online Consumer Habits Research, E-commerce Report 2023. The research showed that 2023 was a year of rationalisation and stagnation in the world of online shopping. In particular, the segment of more frequent monthly online consumers has decreased. Furthermore, Generation Y is coming to the front, becoming the dominant consumer group as their purchasing power increases. The share of cross-border purchases has also increased compared to 2022, while fashion has emerged as the dominant category of online purchases. For more informations 👉: https://lnkd.in/eKnqVJ8k

        The research on online shopping conducted by the companies Valicon and Ceneje! shows that 2023 was also a year of rationalization and stagnation in online retail. 📉🧠🛒 The research results were presented in more detail by David Čreslovnik (Ceneje) and Matjaž Robinšak (Valicon). The former pointed out that consumers will become extremely rational in 2023, leading to a decline in impulse purchases, while Matjaž highlighted the influence of Generation Y, which is increasingly dominating the online market. On the other hand, mobile phones continue to play a leading role in the purchasing process, which shows the constant adaptation of technologies and online platforms to the needs of modern consumers. Other interesting research findings include: ✔Categories such as fashion, electronics and home and garden products have maintained their popularity ✔Domestic purchases continue to dominate, but the proportion of purchases from abroad has increased ✔Most returns concerned clothing, mainly due to incorrect sizes, which shows the importance of accurate product information and presentation on the internet. ✔iPhone and Playstation 5 products were again among the most searched-for products, as were wheelbarrows this year. ✔Despite growth in the fashion segment, this growth has slowed somewhat. On the other hand, products such as electric scooters stand out, reflecting the growing importance of sustainability and ecological awareness among consumers. ✔Important shifts towards sustainable and local production and the use of advanced technologies such as artificial intelligence and chatbots are also predicted for next year. The focus is therefore increasingly on adapting the offering to the individual needs of customers and improving the user experience.
