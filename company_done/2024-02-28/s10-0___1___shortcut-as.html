About us

With a touch, our phone can open a door, turn on the lights and check our health. Our phones and apps are no longer part of the so-called digital world - there is only one world and one life, and it’s quite magical. We are 180+ developers in Oslo, Bergen, Copenhagen, Stockholm, and Bucharest, Ukraine and the Balkans, working closely with customers from all sectors. We put together equal parts of magic and logic to build digital tools used by millions of people every day. Shortcut is where the logic and magic is made. We make apps for life.

    🚨 Some major changes are coming soon in how Apple allows developers to distribute iOS apps and use iPhone capabilities. With these changes, Apple introduces new ways to both pay for and pay with apps. Apple’s recent updates to iOS, Safari, and the App Store in the EU, as per the Digital Markets Act, are a game-changer for European app developers. 🚀 These changes offer enhanced app analytics, over 600 new APIs, and alternative browser engine functionality, significantly expanding developers’ creative and technical horizons. Crucially, developers can now access the iPhone’s NFC chip for payment solutions, paving the way for innovative payment apps and enhanced customer experiences. This move not only diversifies payment options but also fosters a more competitive environment in the EU market. Furthermore, Apple now allows the distribution of iOS apps via alternative channels - like new app stores or specialized app stores. The changes will go into effect as of March 1st. If you are curious about how the new payment or distribution options for iOS apps could benefit your company, feel free to reach out to us for a cup of coffee. Or, for more detailed information, read Apple’s press release from yesterday here 👇
