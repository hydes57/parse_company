      About us

      Organik Nar is a family company whose first fruits are the juicy pomegranates grown on their own plantations spread in an untouched natural environment in the south of Macedonia. After several years of growing pomegranates, the company started producing natural pomegranate juice with a uniquely refreshing taste and authentic aroma. The product range of the ART JUICE brand is based exclusively on the production of fresh fruit juice, without preservatives and additives. Organik Nar produces real juice which is a perfect combination of organically grown fruits and a preparation process according to the highest quality standards.
