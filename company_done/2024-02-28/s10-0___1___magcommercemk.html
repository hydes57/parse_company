About us

MAG Commerce was established in 1994. Started by equipping small retail stores and warehouses. From year to year, we are refreshing and refined, and with each new project gaining, we are new knowledge. Today MAG Commerce offers a wide range of products in the field of metal furniture, shopping trolleys, entrance and exit systems, checkouts, and other accessories for supermarkets, discount stores, boutiques, perfumeries, pharmacies … Our professional team of installers is constantly upgraded attending various trainings. Depending on the type and scope of the assignment, we carry out, communicate or organize the final installation of shop fittings for our customers. We supply and install equipment throughout the country and abroad, where our experienced teams of highly skilled professionals guarantee fast and efficient installation of shop fittings in your store. The desire to create beautiful and functional objects helped us to gain the trust of a growing number of satisfied customers, and thus develop our network of products and services. Today we are official representatives of several international brands whose confidence is based on long-standing mutual cooperation and joint projects carried out successfully. Design of any type of retail facilities and warehouses, tracking trends allow us to be one step ahead of the competition. Wishes of our clients are our challenge for modern facilities. MAG Commerce continues to monitor global trends and developments in its field. Our goal is successful and satisfied customers!
