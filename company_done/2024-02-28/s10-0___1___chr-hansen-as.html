About us

Chr. Hansen has combined with Novozymes to become Novonesis: linkedin.com/company/novonesis. This global biosolutions company of 10,000 people is transforming how the world produces, consumes and lives. In 30-plus industries, its biosolutions help businesses meet their needs and the needs of our planet. Chr. Hansen was founded in 1874 by the Danish pharmacist Christian D. A. Hansen who invented an industrial process to extract rennet for cheese making. The company was a microbial powerhouse that developed natural ingredient solutions for the food, nutritional, pharmaceutical and agricultural industries. Its unique microbial and fermentation technology platforms and collection of around 50,000 microbial strains helped it meet its commitment to grow a better world. Naturally. In December 2022, Chr. Hansen and Novozymes entered into an agreement to create a leading global biosolutions partner by combining the two companies. The goal was to unleash the full potential of biosolutions and generate significant value for all stakeholders and society. The combination was completed on January 29, 2024, and Novonesis was born. The name means ‘a new beginning’ and is derived from the Greek word ‘genesis’. It reflects a new era where we’ll better our world with biology. You can find Novonesis on LinkedIn here: linkedin.com/company/novonesis. Chr. Hansen A/S, part of Novonesis Group.

    Happy New Year! 2024 has arrived, a monumental year for Chr. Hansen marking our 150th anniversary as well as a new beginning as part of Novonesis (pending final authority approval).   In 1874, the company was founded on a groundbreaking discovery that revolutionized the industrial dairy production in the making. Interestingly, the same product – purified, standardized animal rennet – is still in our portfolio today.   Roughly speaking, the next turning point took place a hundred years later with the invention of DVS® – direct vat set – cultures. This allowed dairies to pour concentrated starter cultures directly into the milk, saving time and costs and ensuring excellent, uniform quality of their finished product.   The new millennium saw profound changes involving new ownership, new business areas, a new corporate strategy strongly committed to sustainability and, ultimately, the announcement in late 2022 of the proposed combination with Novozymes to form a leading, global biosolutions partner.   As we approach Day 1 of this combination, let’s pay tribute to the legacy we are bringing into the future. If you’re curious, have a look at our historic timeline (https://lnkd.in/d9Fj8VwG) featuring special highlights throughout our 150 years of existence.   Cheers to former colleagues and business partners who were part of making Chr. Hansen what it is today – and what it will become!

    Earlier this week, the biosolutions sector was gathered at the first-ever Biosolutions High Level Summit in Copenhagen, featuring a great line-up of prominent speakers, including Danish Minister for Industry, Business, and Financial Affairs Morten Bødskov, the Danish Minister for Food & Agri, Jacob Jensen, Director of Tænketanken EUROPA Lykke Friis, and our colleague David Zilber, former Noma-chef and fermentation expert, who delivered a keynote speech about the power of fermentation. “The dawn of civilized cultures, cities, societies were based on fermentation of some of humanity’s first domesticated species …. And when microbes come into the picture as solutions to a human problem, they have the abilities that you couldn’t even dream of. They evolve, they problem solve, they iterate,” said David. The breadth and depth of biosolutions were showcased. Fermented foods, plant-based burgers by Matr, biobricks by Mason, biobased fabrics for clothing by Henrik Vibskov and Pond, fermented binder for asphalt Visibuilt. Solutions inspired by nature, based on biomaterials, and developed to address some of the world’s biggest challenges 🌍 The sector is gaining momentum and has moved up on the agenda! Thanks to Alliance for Biosolutions, Novo Nordisk Foundation and DI Bio for uniting such a powerhouse of start-ups, funds, investors, decision-makers, businesses, and scientists committed to shaping a future inspired by nature for nature 🌱
