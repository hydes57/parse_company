      About us

      Daikin is the leading provider of heating, cooling, ventilation, air purification and refrigeration technology. The Daikin Europe group designs, manufactures and brings to market a broad portfolio of products, maintenance services as well as turnkey solutions for residential, commercial and industrial purposes in Europe, Middle East and Africa. Worldwide, we are more than 96,000 employees. Our regional team at Daikin Central Europe with more than 700 colleagues has diverse professional backgrounds. Together with more than 2,500 HVAC-R* partners, we successfully shape our sales and service business in 16 Central and Eastern European countries: Austria, Albania, Bosnia Herzegovina, Bulgaria, Croatia, Czech Republic, Hungary, Kosovo, Montenegro, Moldova, North Macedonia, Poland, Romania, Serbia, Slovakia and Slovenia. We deliver the highest Japanese engineering skills derived from European manufacturing combined with a strong customer orientation. In 2020, Daikin Central Europe was awarded for the eighth time with "Beste Arbeitgeber – Great Place to Work" Award. In 2022 and 2023, we were recognized as Kununu Top Company. How cool is that! *HVAC-R – heating, ventilation, air-conditioning, refrigeration
