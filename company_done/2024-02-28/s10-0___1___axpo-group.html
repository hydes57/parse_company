About us

Axpo is driven by a single purpose – to enable a sustainable future through innovative energy solutions. Axpo is Switzerland's largest producer of renewable energy and an international leader in energy trading and the marketing of solar and wind power. Axpo combines the experience and expertise of more than 6,000 employees who are driven by a passion for innovation, collaboration and impactful change. Using cutting-edge technologies, Axpo innovates to meet the evolving needs of its customers in over 30 countries across Europe, North America and Asia. Around 6.000 employees work for Axpo in 150 different roles – this means a wide range of job entry and career opportunities for you. Bring along your knowledge, your enthusiasm and your dedication – and take charge. Visit our career website: www.axpo.com/career

    💧⚡💡 Axpo joins a Green #Hydrogen Project in #Italy! #Axpo announced its collaboration with Infinite Green Energy on an ambitious green hydrogen initiative. ➡️ Important facts on the project: 🌿 Axpo Group is partnering with IGE to deliver up to 12 tonnes of hydrogen per day. This will significantly contribute to decarbonizing operations in the challenging industrial and transportation sectors. 🌿 The project includes a 30MW #electrolyser, powered by a 45MWp solar plant making it one of Italy’s largest hydrogen production sites. 🌿 #IGE estimates an annual CO2 emissions saving of around 67,000 tonnes. This reduction extends to hard-to-abate industries, reinforcing the project’s environmental impact. 🌿 Hydrogen Valley: Since IGE initiated the project 18 months ago, local authorities have aptly named the area the "Hydrogen Valley".
