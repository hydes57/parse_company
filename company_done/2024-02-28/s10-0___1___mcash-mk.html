      About us

      M Kesh is the first online financial company in our country that offers loans quickly and easily adapted to the needs of the Macedonian market, with over 50 money shops across the country and more than 180 employees. M Cash's portfolio includes various types of products and services in the field of microcredit, including M Free, M Fix, M Safe, M Combo, and M Business. M Cash is part of the Management Financial Group (MFG) holding, which unites the leading providers of non-banking financial services in Central and Eastern Europe and has over 18 years of experience in the markets of Bulgaria, Romania, Poland, Ukraine, the Czech Republic, Croatia, Spain, North Macedonia and other countries. MFG manages a rich portfolio of successful business models in consumer loans, loans for micro and small businesses, credit cards, digital companies, and other alternative financial products and services. It has around 300 offices, over 6,000 employees, and over 1,200,000 issued loans. Values Humanity: We show understanding to people by working openly, closely, with trust and recognition. Innovation: We grown by the energy of youth and childlike curiosity that makes us accessible, innovative, experimental, and challenging ourselves. Responsibility: We are guided by our responsibility towards people - colleagues, partners, customers, and the environment in which we live and which surrounds us. Objectives To provide as fast, easy, and accessible financial service as possible. To become our users' preferred lender, offer transparent, affordable, and quick loans secured through first-class personal and professional services. Let's make M Cash an exciting, inspiring, and rewarding place where people will want to work. This company values and provides opportunities to its teams and employees and cares about their professional development. To be a respected and reliable partner who performs the activity transparent, responsibly, and ethically.
