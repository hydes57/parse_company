About us

We are a successful and dynamic European company called Film Fixers Balkans. Our aim is to make filming on the Balkans an easy and enjoyable experience for every filmmaker no matter what the genre or the budget of your production is! From booking studios and equipment to finding a place to stay for a reasonable price in any part of East Europe, from employing extra crew members to providing the catering for your sets - Film Fixers Balkans will do it for you while you concentrate on creating a cinematic masterpiece! Having worked with such companies as BBC, Fremantle Media, Boundless, National Geographic, RDF Television and E! We can proudly say, If you ever are in need of help when filming on the Balkans, we are right behind you! For more information please feel free to contact us at info@filmfixersbalkans.com.
