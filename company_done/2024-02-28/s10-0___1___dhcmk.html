About us

The National association for digitalisation of health and care â€“ Digital Health and Care Macedonia was founded in November 2022 as a non-profit, non-governmental organisation. The initiative was inspired by the crumbling public healthcare system, diminishing of the public healthcare services, and excessive migration of healthcare professionals due to poor working conditions, to other countries or transiting to the private healthcare sector. Therefore, an enormous gap to fill is present in order to advance the health of individuals and their communities. Digital health solutions, as a powerful tool in combating inequities in healthcare services delivery and accessibility as a basic human right, hold the potential to partially solve the current problem with the aim to facilitate access and timeliness in the delivery of services to (underserved) communities, with emphasis on those with limited supply and/or access to services due to location. Our mission is to promote the biopsychosocial health and well-being of all citizens through implementation of digital health solutions, by using patient-centric, holistic and salutogenic approach supported by multisectoral activities and cooperation between responsible bodies and institutions. As the first organization of its kind in the country, DHCMK will lead the digitalisation of health and social care by identifying systemâ€™s deficiencies and undertaking activities in the appropriate domains for their optimization.
