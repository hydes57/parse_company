About us

The PMI N. Macedonia Chapter is not-for-profit membership association for the project management profession in North Macedonia. The chapter aims to develop and promote global standards in project management. The chapter has been an integral part of the Project Management Institute (PMI), the foremost international association of project managers. Founded in 1969, PMI is now the largest and most prestigious project management association in the world, with over half a million members from 185 countries.
