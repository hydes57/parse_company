About us

Endeavor is the company that is mainly focused on creating strategies for the growth and development of companies in the digital world, dispersed in different industries and different spheres of activity. The purpose of the company is to provide a complete product in one place, from programming, testing, product management, design and marketing of the product. It is oriented towards the European market with an accent on the Baltic countries. A compact company that aims not only at creating financial results, but also at the growth and development of the employees and clients in the regions where it works.
