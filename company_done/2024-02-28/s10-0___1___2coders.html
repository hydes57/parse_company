      About us

      2Coders Studio is a custom software development company driven by a creative and meaningful approach to creating digital solutions with the users in mind. We specialize in Native mobile apps and OTT platforms (Connected TV apps & web). Back in 2013, 2Coders started as an idea shared by 2 friends (tech nerds) united by one mission — to help world-renowned brands reach millions of users through digital transformation. Today, we are a diverse team of Digital Artisans in all tech & creative fields, fulfilling that mission every day. From experienced mobile, web, and TV engineers, UX/UI designers, to QAs, and product specialists, we help you build your custom Apps and OTT platforms from idea to launch. What we offer: Native mobile Apps We are experts in developing native iOS and Android apps, as they allow an optimal usability and performance. Our expertise includes sectors such as video streaming, tourism or industry. OTT platforms/ Connected TV Apps We help you reach your audience on every device by developing top-quality Connected TV applications to deploy across Smart TVs, Set-Top-Boxes, gaming consoles, web and mobile devices. Digital Transformation & Innovation Consulting We analyze your business model, strategy and goals to optimize your processes, providing the most appropriate technological solution. Our offering includes ERP, CRM, B2B websites and eCommerce custom software solutions. Staff augmentation We are experts in building and operating software development teams that will seamlessly integrate with your in-house team thanks to the implementation of agile methodologies.
