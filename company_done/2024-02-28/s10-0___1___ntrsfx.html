      About us

      NTRSFX - NOTORIOUS EFFECTS is a Marketing, Video & Design Agency that helps individuals and businesses grow, scale, create and execute effective campaigns for their brand. The services that NTRSFX offers are: 1. Complete Digital Marketing: Developing and implementing marketing strategies that align with the overall business goals, funnel building, lead generating, data analysis, SEO, SMO, campaigns to launch, brand awareness and acquisition, and more. 2. Video Production & Editing: NTRSFX produces high-quality, trending video content that can be used for variety of purposes such as marketing, promotional content, documentaries, podcasts, educational content, VFX, product demos, employee training, entertainment, etc. 3. Graphic & Motion Design: Unique Brand Identity, logo, banner, infographic, flyer/brochure, visit card, menus, thumbnails, carousel, GIF, CV design-redesign, Instagram Story/Post design, Instagram Grid design, merchandize, podcast design, app or software design, cover, poster, website design and the list goes on. 4. Website CMS - Odoo & Wordpress: With expertise in Odoo ERP & Wordpress, building complete websites with ease. Compelling, modern, responsive websites that lay the foundation of your brand. 5. Social Media Management: While you work on other projects and campaigns, NTRSFX manages your complete Social Media presence and brand reputation. All of the channels – posting content, responding to prospects, answering questions, engaging with the community and scheduling of content.
