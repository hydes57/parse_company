      About us

      Itecor is an international IT boutique consultancy headquartered in Switzerland. As a trusted advisor, we bring our customers' visions to life through digital transformation, offering services in: • Governance & Service Management • Digital Solutions • Quality Assurance & Testing. Thanks to our communities of practice, we provide our people with the support they need to become the best in their field. Our local and cross-cutting teams of experts build sustainable relationships with customers from various areas, including technology, banking and finance, consumer goods, the public sector, and education. Close to our clients, delivering high-value services, embodying applied innovation and empowering humans: this is the core of who we are.
