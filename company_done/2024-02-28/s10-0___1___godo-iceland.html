About us

GODO is a travel tech company offering B2B solutions for the hospitality industry. Our leading product is the Travia marketplace. Travia enables B2B buyers (e.g. travel agencies) to directly contract hotels and accommodation providers with no middlemen and process live reservations directly to over 700 hotel systems. Travia is an API-first platform.
