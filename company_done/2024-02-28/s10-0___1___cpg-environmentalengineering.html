About us

Established in 2002, CPG Environmental Engineering, a proud subsidiary of CPG International Pte Ltd, is a prominent force in the industrial environmental engineering landscape. CPG Environmental Engineering specializes in one-stop turnkey solution of integrated systems for Environmental Engineering Services, with a primary focus on Industrial Water Treatment Systems. With a rich history of serving internationally renowned clients across various industrial sectors worldwide, we are committed to exceeding our clients' expectations by delivering projects within budget, on schedule, and to the highest quality standards. As a Design and Build Water Systems specialist, we offer a comprehensive range of services, from Conceptual Engineering and Design, Fabrication and Installation, to Equipment and System Testing & Commissioning. Our Comprehensive Environmental Engineering Services include: • Industrial Wastewater Treatment and Wastewater Recycling • Industrial Demineralized Water and Softened Water Solutions • Seawater Desalination • Zero Liquid Discharge, and more. Our impressive track record has earned us the recognition and affirmation of leading international companies, thanks to our customised solutions, cutting-edge technology, consistent service and product quality. We have proudly served clients in various industrial sectors, including: • Pharmaceutical Industry • Automotive Industry • Electro-plating and Surface Treatment Industry • Food & Beverage Industry • Whiskey/ Wine/ Beer Distillery Industry • Chemical Industry • Steel Industry • Semiconductors and Printed Circuit Board Industry • Textile Industry • Paper and Pulp Industry Join us on LinkedIn to stay updated on our latest projects, industry insights, and environmental engineering innovations. Discover how CPG Environmental Engineering is making a positive impact on industries worldwide through sustainable and efficient water treatment solutions.

      In 2015, Eli Lilly and Company (Lilly), a US-based pharmaceutical giant headquartered in Indianapolis, Indiana, strategically expanded its global footprint by establishing its 3rd insulin plant in Suzhou Industrial Park, China. This move aimed to capitalize on the burgeoning diabetes treatment market in the country.   The newly constructed facility, designed specifically for insulin filling, commenced operations in 2017. Notably, CPG Environmental Engineering Co., Ltd. (CPG-EE) was entrusted with the wastewater treatment system package in 2015 and effectively completed the project by the end of 2016. Our responsibilities encompassed the full spectrum, including design, procurement, fabrication, supply, testing, and commissioning of the wastewater treatment system. The project involved managing both phosphorus-containing and non-phosphorus-containing wastewater.   To enhance the user-friendliness of the wastewater system, CPG-EE implemented a value engineering design, introducing an integrated polymer makeup and dosing system. Leveraging the Prominent Ultromat system, a fully automated polyelectrolyte preparation system, not only streamlined chemical preparation but also ensured precise polymer batching and dosing. These processes are automated and programmable to desired concentration settings, reducing operator workload.   Installation posed a challenge due to tight space constraints allotted for the wastewater treatment system. Adhering to our Zero Harm Policy, the project management team and #HealthSafetyandEnvironment (#HSE) officer collaborated closely to execute the work safely and without hazards. Simultaneously, the compressed schedule, driven by the Lilly's urgency to have the plant operational by a specific date, added complexity. Despite these challenges, the CPG-EE's project team triumphed, delivering a #cuttingedge wastewater treatment system with exceptional quality finishes to Eli Lilly and Company.   Since 2017, the wastewater treatment system has been operational, contributing to Eli Lilly's commitment to #sustainability and #environmentalresponsibility. #WastewaterTreatmentSystem #IndustrialWaterTreatment #Pharmaceutical

      Delighted to share our accomplishment at CPG Environmental Engineering (CPG-EE) in partnership with DURA Automotive Systems. DURA, a renowned designer and manufacturer of automotive components, entrusted us with their pivotal Greenfield project in Skopje, Macedonia, focused on developing a Wastewater Treatment System and Demineralized Water System. Amidst the complexities of the COVID-19 pandemic, DURA set an ambitious delivery timeline for April 2021, a challenge we took on with determination. This endeavor presented formidable hurdles, particularly navigating supply chain disruptions with escalating material costs and sea-freight expenses post-contract signing. Despite these obstacles, our team at CPG-EE leveraged innovative strategies, notably by breaking down the wastewater treatment and demineralized water systems into smaller, manageable submodule systems. These submodules were pre-assembled into skids before being transported to the job site, significantly reducing on-site installation time. Our unwavering dedication and creative problem-solving enabled us to successfully deliver the project to DURA Automotive within the stipulated timeframe. The result: a cutting-edge wastewater treatment system and demineralized water system with a premium quality finish, achieved in April 2021. Notably, CPG-EE completed this complex undertaking within a mere 7-months from the issuance of the Purchase Order by DURA Automotive. This accomplishment stands as a testament to our commitment to excellence and our ability to surmount challenges with innovative solutions. It was a privilege to collaborate with DURA Automotive Systems, and we look forward to continuing to deliver value and innovation in our projects. #WastewaterTreatmentSystem #DemineralizedWaterSystem #CPGEEGlobal #EngineeringExcellence #InnovationInAction #CollaborativeSuccess
