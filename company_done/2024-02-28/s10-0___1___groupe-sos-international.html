About us

Groupe SOS is a global leader of the social and solidarity economy. Since our creation in 1984 in France, we innovate for the most vulnerable people, for future generations and for underserved territories. Impact is our compass: we have zero shareholder, our 22.000 employees only serve millions of beneficiaries, in 40+ countries. We support hyper-local capacity building: · Planète Urgence 🌱 · Santé Sud 👩⚕️ · PLAY International 🤼 · Afghanistan Libre 📚 · Schools of Bienfaisance 🎒 We catalyze green and social entrepreneurship: · PULSE Incubators 💡 · Impact Business Angels 💸 · People Power Inclusion (PPI) 🤝 · Ateliere Fara Frontiere ♻️ We drive systemic change: · Impact Tank 💬 · Groupe SOS Consulting 🎯 · French Impact 🚀 · Impact Campus 🎓 Find out more about Groupe SOS’ international activities here!
