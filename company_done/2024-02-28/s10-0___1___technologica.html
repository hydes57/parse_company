About us

TechnoLogica is a leading Bulgarian technology company. Our motto is We make IT work for you! Information technology is simply a tool, which can help you make the best business decisions and implement them in practice. We select these tools and make them work for you, finding the best ways to do it in light of your goals. It is our business to be the technological partner to prestigious companies and organizations and help them implement and take advantage of new information technologies in their projects and R&D. To stay competitive as the provider of technology transfer services TechnoLogica follows a target-oriented policy of innovation by anticipatory implementation, adaptation and further development of new information technologies and generation of technological software tools. TechnoLogica offers both software products and a range of services that complement the development and implementation of various information systems: - Custom software development - transaction systems,geographic information systems, data warehousing (DW) and business intelligence (BI); - HeRMeS ® - own human resource management software products; - Security solutions - cyber security, crisis and accident management, command and control solutions; - Business software and services - business process modeling and analysis, project management and GDPR services; - 3D solutions - from the idea to the finished product; - Implementation of information systems – all activities related to the research and analysis of the existing situation and definition of the requirements for the software solutions, design, implementation, development and maintenance; - Payroll outsourcing; - Education Center - specialized training in the field of Oracle technologies, Cyber security, Project management, Business Process Modeling and Analysis.

HeRMeS eXpress

HeRMeS eXpress

Human Resources Management Systems (HRMS)

HeRMeS eXpress е цялостно решение за управление на човешки ресурси за малък и среден бизнес. То ще трансформира вашите HR процеси и ще създаде уникално служителско изживяване с интуитивен Self-Service портал. HeRMeS eXpress е облачно решение, създадено от ТехноЛогика, базирано на 25-годишния опит на компанията в разработването и внедряването на софтуер за управление на човешки ресурси. С HeRMeS eXpress дигитализирате и управлявате на едно място цялата информация за вашите служители. Ефективно, удобно и автоматизирано.
