      The words don’t stop coming, so we’re updating the dictionary more frequently than ever! And not just with any words: this update includes an incredibly useful concentration of terms for naming the complexities of modern life. We’ll break down the words that are new to the dictionary, newly added, or newly revised (such as the AI sense of "hallucinate"). Keep in mind that words that are new to the dictionary are not always new to the language (or even remotely recent), but their growth and change is just as interesting! We can’t endorse any word, but we can document their use in the real world. We are descriptive—we describe language as it is really used (not just how we or others may wish it would be used). Learn which words have been added and updated at Dictionary.com!
