About us

We are an agile software development consultancy with a mission. We shape the future of premium remote software development services. Our humble goal is to build the best workplace in Croatia and the Balkan region for developers who love to craft kick-ass digital experiences for our clients - with a Nordic twist. Software Sauna was founded by two Finnish friends who wanted to combine the Nordic business culture with expertise of top notch engineers in the SEE area. We provide Full Stack software development in agile modern web and mobile technologies. Our fundamental principles are Quality and Transparency. Our team of senior developers with 5 to 15+ years of experience in software engineering has solid knowledge of various programming languages and frameworks. We operate as your remote development team according to your current needs.
