About us

In a rapidly changing business environment, new challenges are facing businesses whether Startups, SMEs, or Large Organizations. In Pinnacle® we believe that every organization big or small has potentials within, it just needs to be revealed and inspired to reach its performance pinnacle; that is why Pinnacle was founded, to build great people to do extraordinary things. Pinnacle® Ltd is a consultancy firm focusing on people, technology and process performance enhancement; with Pinnacle® collectively experienced multinational consultants, best in class technology, and lean six sigma process approaching business challenges; we deliver value added resulting in your people and business growth.
