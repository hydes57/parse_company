About us

BtoBet, part of Neogames group, is a leading B2B provider of sports betting software, solutions and services to leading Tier 1 operators and newer brands. Its cutting-edge sportsbook platform, which is ISO 27001 and GLI-33 certified and is trusted by partners in 4 continents, is compliant for the UK, Poland, Colombia, Argentina, Mexico, Venezuela, Jamaica, Nigeria, Kenya, Rwanda, Madagascar, Senegal, Ghana, Congo Brazzaville, Sierra Leone, Zambia, Democratic Republic of Congo, Chad, Mozambique, Tanzania, Guinea, Namibia, Angola. The sportsbook platform is also made available to operators via Aspire Global in a host of European jurisdiction including Ireland, Portugal, Denmark, Malta, Germany, and the Netherlands. BtoBet is focused on providing its partners with premium technological solution, enabling them to deliver stimulating player experiences in regulated markets.
