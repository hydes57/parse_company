      About us

      Stavreska Recruiting is a global recruitment agency that specializes in helping remote-first startups and scaleups find the right employees to fit their unique culture. At Stavreska Recruiting, we understand that time and money are valuable resources for startups, which is why we strive to make the recruitment process as efficient, cost-effective, and hassle-free as possible. Our team of experienced recruiters will analyze your job description, culture, and hiring needs to ensure that we are finding the best fit for your company. We will screen and interview candidates, and prevent common hiring mistakes such as hiring someone who does not align with your values. In addition to our recruitment services, Stavreska Recruiting also offers training to help your in-house team learn how to evaluate and conduct effective interviews. This training will allow your team to take control of the recruitment process and make informed hiring decisions. So, if you are a startup looking to save time and money during the recruitment process, look no further than Stavreska Recruiting. Let our team of experts help you find the right fit for your company's culture. Contact us at contact@stavreska.com
