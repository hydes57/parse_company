About us

Heads Adriatic is a regional holding company that specializes in HR consultancy services, offering professional solutions via niche expertise. Through our specialized brands we are able to provide an end-to-end tailored service to our clients, while securing the best opportunities for our candidates. Heads Adriatic has regional offices in 12 locations and has more than 20 years of hands-on market experience with a single goal – to help our partners be the best at hiring and managing their talent. Our core solutions: Heads Talent Solutions; Talent Acquisition Workforce; Recruitment & Staffing Qonnexa; Business Process Solutions MJob; Youth Employment

    CMS Belgrade recently hosted a meeting of AHK Serbia's HR Committee, where members discussed current topics of interest for businesses in Serbia and exchanged experience and ideas. Our legal experts – Dragana Bajic and Jovan Bećirić - shared insights on the latest regulatory changes and issues they may bring. The meeting covered important topics - the draft Law on Internship, the new procedure for hiring foreign citizens, and the new Law on Dual Education. We would like to thank German-Serbian Chamber of Commerce (AHK Serbien) for organising this meeting and for their continuous efforts to ensure that all members are well-informed about the latest developments impacting professionals and businesses in Serbia.
