    Cyber Security Workshop #scenarioplanning #gamedevelopment - #cybersecurity workshop with Anti-Corruption Commission. March 16, a cyber security workshop was held for the employees of the professional service of Anti-corruption Commission, organized by the IMPETUS Association - Center for Internet, Development and Good Governance, and with the support of the Foreign, Commonwealth and Development Office and the DCAF - Geneva Centre for Security Sector Governance. Employees had the opportunity to learn about cyber threats, risks, challenges, as well as tools for protection and prevention. At the same time, they had the opportunity in an interactive way, through a game and through a discussion, to practice scenarios of cyber attacks how to react and what to watch out for. #cyber #integrity #cybersecurity

    International conference "Cyber Integrity in Public Administration", organized by Impetus with huge and generous support from Foreign, Commonwealth and Development Office, British Embassy Skopje and DCAF - Geneva Centre for Security Sector Governance. The event enabled the sharing of #keymessages and #recommendations with all relevant stakeholders in order to more easily deal with cyber security threats and encourage transformative changes. 160 participants from about 50 different institutions from executive, judicial and local government, with panelists from the Western Balkans Chevening Awards on Cybersecurity Fellowship and private sector from Great Britain. #administration #education #cybersecurity #event #cyber Special thanks goes to Cranfield University, Bec McKeown CPsychol, Tim P., Adam Zagorecki, Petar Backovic, Eriola Sadiku, Marina Jovanovska, Driart Elshani, Visar Shllaku, Predrag Puharic, Dijana Milutinović, Natalija Radoja, Franziska Klopfer, Laura Thaqi Gratitude to the Deputy Prime Minister Slavica Grkovska who supported the event, MIOA Minister Azir Aliu for the key messages and recommendations, as well as the Director of the Innovation and Technological Development Fund Festim Halili for the innovative proposed digital assistant solutions, and the President of the Anti-corruption Commission, Biljana Ivanovska.
