About us

Hello, we are AB Novoselski, a company that’s been established for over 20 years on the market. We have stores in 4 cites in Macedonia, including our 4000 m2 distribution warehouse in Strumica – house to more than 10000 parts and 20 staff. We sell car and truck parts and accessories, from mechanical items to consumables, for both trade and public customers. Our philosophy is to provide “Parts for every vehicle” – not just at low price but also at the highest possible quality. We stock parts from all the major parts manufacturers, which ensures we have what you require. Also we specialize in workshop consumables, garage and diagnostic equipment and specialist hand tool.
