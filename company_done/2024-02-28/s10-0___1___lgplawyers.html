      About us

      With more than 100 employees and lawyers from more than 20 countries, LGP Lawyers ranks amongst the biggest business law firms in Austria and Slovakia. The firm, with offices in Austria, Slovakia, North Macedonia, Kazakhstan, Ras Al Khaimah, UAE, Turkey and Czech Republic, has many years of in-depth experience and offers its clients individual, strategically conceived and pragmatic complete solutions. LGP works closely with external specialists and has a global, cross-border network of advisers. The firm's consciously chosen, integrated approach is quite distinct from the general standard of conventional, purely legal advice. Our decisive added value for you is LGP’s approach of "Being right by thinking laterally". By this we mean the ability to combine everything at our disposal in order to find the best solution. For you as our client, this means ensuring that your interests are represented by lawyers who can think across all industries, disciplines and systems – combined with pragmatism. Almost one third of the lawyers at LGP previously gained professional experience in highly specialised subjects – from medicine to journalism and music. They demonstrate this practical knowledge in their work as lawyers. That's what unconventional ingredients for the most successful solution look like! You can count on our network that extends far beyond the borders of Austria when we represent your interests at the intersection of business, media and politics. If necessary, we employ other advisers or service providers in Austria and abroad to help solve your problem – from investment consultants to private investigators and civil engineers. This approach enables us to prevent some legal problems even before they arise and also to solve the most complicated of cases.

        New year, new opportunities for the start-up scene 🚀   At the end of last year, the Federal Act on Flexible Companies (Flexible Kapitalgesellschafts-Gesetz, "FlexKapGG") as part of the Corporate Law Amendment Act 2023 (Gesellschaftsrechts-Änderungsgesetz 2023, "GesRÄG 2023"), has been adopted and became applicable as of 1 January 2024. With the Act a new corporate form “Flexible Company” or #FlexCo with a minimum share capital of EUR 10.000 has been introduced in Austria.   #FlexCo has been tailored to offer young and innovative start-ups more flexibility by - among others - reducing numerous formalities that apply to limited liability companies, introducing new form of participation for employees through so-called enterprise value shares as well as providing certain tax advantages.   We are already thrilled to evidence the long awaited developments in the Austrian start-up scene very soon. However, not to forget, also existing businesses may transform their corporate form from a limited liability company or a joint stock company into a #FlexCo. We will be happy to assist our clients with the transition into a #FlexCo. In order to gain profound knowledge and practical experience with the new corporate form, LGP Lawyers will start their own project soon.   STAY TUNED ⏳
