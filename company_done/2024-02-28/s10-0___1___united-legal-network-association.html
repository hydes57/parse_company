About us

The United Legal Network Association was founded in 2022 in order to strengthen and enhance the relationship of members of the ULN EEIG, created in 1999, further and to take the next step in broadening the network with new members and clients. So far, the Association consists of 21 legal professionals from 20 different countries and is consistently growing. We offer small and medium sized commercial law firms the opportunity of working internationally and offering their clients representation and expertise cross-border. ULN aims to build a vibrant international community of lawyers that offers mutual support, meaningful connections, professional opportunities and legal excellence.
