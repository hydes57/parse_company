About us

Sopro offers a B2B outreach service that combines cutting-edge technology, world-class data, and a team of 300 experts to engage and nurture your potential clients. Our intent-based multi-channel outreach pairs relentless innovation with buyer intent signals to deliver the right message to the right person, in the right place, at the right time. But don't just take our word for it. With over 22,000 campaigns delivered for 2,600 businesses worldwide, our track record speaks for itself. Whether you're an ambitious start-up or a multinational enterprise, our scalable solution allows you to focus on what you do best, while we drive sales enquiries straight to your inbox. We’ll help you unlock the full potential of your sales pipeline with our unparalleled prospecting service. Check out our latest whitepaper: The State of Prospecting 2024 - https://bit.ly/49kYvE9

            The Mental Availability theory 🧠 ✨ 👉 What is it? According to Byron Sharp's "How Brands Grow" and the Ehrenberg-Bass Institute, brand love and loyalty take a backseat to "mental availability". Simply put, buyers prefer brands that come to mind effortlessly. 👉 How does it apply to marketing? Marketing works by leaving subtle reminders about your brand. These reminders build up over time and lead to more sales, especially when using big-scale marketing strategies. 👉 How does it apply to sales outreach? Multi-channel prospecting makes sure your brand sticks in people's minds and is easy to find when prospects are ready to buy. By tracking buyer intent and matching your messages with what buyers are looking for, you become easy to remember and find. ---------------------------------- 📊 The results Research carried out by the B2B institute at LinkedIn shows the results this can have in practice. Buyers who were previously exposed to brand messages were 36% more likely to accept an InMail from a salesperson and 24% more likely to connect with them. Want more prospecting insights like this?
