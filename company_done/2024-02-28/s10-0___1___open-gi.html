About us

We are a leading technology provider supporting the general insurance market. As a software partner, we have a strong reputation for reliable, robust platforms. Consequently, our software has been widely used and trusted by brokers, insurers, MGAs and underwriting agencies across the UK and RoI for over 40 years. Our head office is based in Worcestershire and we are part of the Open International Group. Today, the Group employs over 500 people with additional offices located throughout the UK and Europe in Winchester, Milton Keynes, Skopje and Krakow.
