      About us

      Gugoska Law Office provides legal services in the following areas: Corporate: Registration of legal entities, mergers and acquisitions, company restructuring, assisting in adoption of statutes, rulebooks, auditing compliance, and responding to civil, administrative or criminal actions, providing business consultations, tax and transactional management consultation, litigation. Property and real-estate management: Assisting investors, real estate businesses, commercial leases, litigation. Employment, benefits and Labor Law: Representing clients in employment litigations, providing consultation and assistance in HR related areas, drafting a complete package of internal company documentation, drafting disciplinary rulebooks and navigating disciplinary procedure, drafting employment and Managerial contracts. Insurance, through providing consultation regarding life insurance, investment portfolios and non life insurance.
