About us

SALON 29 is a Limited Liability Company , founded in 1989. Its main business activity is interior design and office furniture for business premises. It mostly cooperates with internationally well known furniture manufacturers , such as: DELLA ROVERE, FULL MOBILI, LAS MOBILI, CERNTOLA, DESIGN OFFICE, DVO, SEPOINT , BRADO, ABOUT OFFICE etc. At the beginning, it started as a retail office furniture shop, to grow gradually into office furniture distributor on the whole territory of Republic of Macedonia and wider in the Balkans, and now it is one of the biggest distributors of office furniture imported from Italy. Presently, SALON 29 , located in Skopje , disposes with the biggest office furniture show room in Macedonia, covering 300m2, situated very near to the center of the city. The furniture and the chairs distributed by us possess all the internationally recognized quality certificates, as well as the required warranties and spare parts. The main parameters that serve as guidelines for our work team are preciseness and transparency toward our clients, maintenance of adequate spare parts inventory and creating a design space that provides happy work ambient.
