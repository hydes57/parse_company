About us

NAYA Life Sciences stands as a leading manufacturer and supplier of contamination control solutions for life science industries worldwide. With over 35 years of experience and a dedicated team of more than 200 qualified employees, we specialize in providing turnkey cleanroom solutions and (bio)process technology for (bio)pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, healthcare, and other research and development industries. Headquartered in Ljubljana, with branch offices in Istanbul, Skopje, Limburg and Turku, we proudly serve customers in Europe, the USA, and the Middle East. Our mission is to enable any company or research institution to accelerate breakthrough technology by providing flexible and high-quality contamination control solutions.
