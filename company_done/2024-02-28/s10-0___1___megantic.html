      About us

      Megantic is the only Organic Search (SEO) specialist agency in Australia dedicated to eCommerce. We have created a unique strategy for online retailers which enables your website to target educated customers that are traditionally ignored, and substantially grow your online revenue. We are multi-award winners in SEO across a variety of categories in Australia, APAC, UK and Globally. With four offices over the globe (UK, Macedonia, Canada and flagship office in Australia), we have educated experts in multiple international markets. Our team of technically minded specialists live and breath SEO and have a sole focus on this channel which has enabled us to master it and produce exceptional results. Our strategy is proven and tested in the marketplace - it is a holistic strategy that raises onsite and offsite marketing practices and has a focus on targeting your full marketplace online. All of our work is in line with SEO best practices and Google's guidelines. Our strategy is designed for long term growth to help grow and secure your market share.

        Last week, Kristina Trood was selected as a mentor in our program with The Mac.Robertson Girls' High School and ran a session on #SEO. After discussing a definition of SEO, Kristina brought out her favourite analogy: If you think about the internet like a library, and all the websites on the www are like books. When you look up a book in the library's catalogue, you use 'search terms' or 'queries' and the library's system uses an algorithm to search all the text in the books and bring up the most relevant ones in a list. This is like when you perform a search on Google - but their algorithm quickly screens all websites and pulls the top 10 that it thinks are the most relevant into the top 10 positions. Onsite, offsite and technical SEO help to get your website into the top 10. Finally, Kristina reviewed some of our current client's websites, pointing out areas where SEO adjustments were made, including URLs, metadata and filters. Looking ahead, our next session will cover keyword research and website structure. Mentors: Emily Bencsics, Frezel Enriquez, Dale Underwood & Xavier Davis

          Yesterday, we invited a select group of students from The Mac.Robertson Girls' High School for the fourth year of our mentorship program.
