About us

Contiinex helps listen to your customer better through its industry specialist speech-to-text engine and LLMs that can be deployed on customer private cloud instances. Our speech AI platform is designed to pick up on the nuances of speech that a human ear might miss. We believe that this is a very valuable information that our customers can use to improve their customer service and get to the next. As a team we take pride in the fact that each one of us in the Contiinex tribe are supremely dedicated to their craft of unravelling the speech mystery, fine tuning ourselves to go deeper and deeper into the realm of speech and solving for more and more of business use cases. And then we celebrate every time someone gets to The Next version of themselves or helps company get to the Next level. So where do you wanna go Next?
